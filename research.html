Supervision

I supervise postgraduate and undergraduate projects, primarily focusing on the following topics:

  • Software Engineering: Models, Methods, and Tools for optimizing the development of mobile and distributed systems
  • Educational Technology: Developing app-supported interventions for engagement and education (see Code Cyprus)
  • Other Topics: I occasionally work and have published work on topics such as: Machine Learning, Indoor Positioning, Gamification, etc.

Research Grants

I am currently involved in the following European or Nationally funded project proposals and/or implementations:
  • PREPARED (2022-2025) - funded by Horizon Europe (Grant agreement ID: 101058094) - design and develop software tools to enhance project outreach and impact (€4.2M)
  • MENTORme (2020-2023) - funded by Erasmus+ (2020-1-PL01-KA203-081667) - designed and developed software tools to facilitate training (296K)
  • iREEDER (2019-2022) - funded by Erasmus+ (609971-EPP-1-2019-1-JO-EPPKA2-CBHE-JP) - developed IoT learning material (897 KEURO)
In the past, I have been involved in the following European or Nationally funded project proposals and/or implementations:
  • RegTek (2020-2022) - funded by the Research and Innovation Foundation under RESTART 2020 Innovate call - developed Machine Learning and Data Analytics algorithms and software for fraud detection (€1M)
  • SLICES-DS (2020-2022) - funded by Horizon 2020 (Grant agreement ID: 951850) - developed a Design Study for Scientific Large-scale Infrastructure for Computing/Communication Experimental Studies (€2.9M)
  • ML-FraudDetect (2017-2018) - funded by the Research and Innovation Foundation under RESTART 2020 Innovation Vouchers call (€5K)
  • RRI MobDev (2017) - selected for funding by the Research and Innovation Foundation under RESTART 2020 Cyprus/France bilateral collaboration call (€10K)
  • Wearable Robots (2017-2021) Cost Action 16116 - formed a European network of experts to develop a new generation of Wearable Robots, characterized by better adjustment to the human users (traveling budget only)
  • Responsible Industry (2014-2017) - funded by Framework Programme 7 (Grant agreement ID: 609817) - designed and implemented research methods to develop an Exemplar Implementation Plan of RRI in Industry (€1.5M)
  • ACROSS (2013-2017) Cost Action IC1304 - formed a European network of experts to develop autonomous control methods and algorithms for a reliable and quality-aware IoS (traveling budget only)
Also, while at the University of Cyprus I was involved in the following project proposals and/or implementations:
  • ICT-AsTeRICS (2010-2012) - funded by the EU Framework Programme 7 (Grant agreement ID: 247730) - main contributor to the proposal writeup and work package lead designing the core architecture of the OSGi-based middleware (€3.4M)
  • IST-MUSIC (2006-2010) - funded by the EU Framework Programme 6 (Grant agreement ID: 035166) - main contributor to the proposal writeup and work package lead (€8.7M)
  • IST-MADAM (2004-2007) - funded by the EU Framework Programme 5 (Grant agreement ID: 004159) - participated as developer and work package lead (€2.6M)
