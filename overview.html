I am an assistant professor in computing at the University of Central Lancashire — Cyprus.

My research lies in the areas of software engineering and distributed systems. I am specializing in methods for context-aware, self-adaptive applications and services, with emphasis on mobile and pervasive computing environments.

I maintain the School of Sciences' Seminars & Events blog.

We are delighted that our partners have supported Code Cyprus which is repeating this year with the aim of encouraging high school students to learn programming.
