I am an assistant professor in computing at the University of Central Lancashire — Cyprus.

My main research lies in the areas of Software Engineering and Distributed Systems. More precisely, I specialize in methods for Context-aware, Self-adaptive applications and services, with emphasis on Mobile and Pervasive computing environments.
I am also active in the areas of: Responsible Research and Innovation (RRI) in the software industry, Computer Science Education (see Code Cyprus), and Gamification as a means for student engagement and behavior change.

I maintain the School of Sciences' Seminars & Events blog.

I am the founder of Code Cyprus which is a yearly event aiming to encourage high school students to learn programming.
