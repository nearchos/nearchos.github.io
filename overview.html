I am an assistant professor in computing at the University of Central Lancashire — Cyprus.

My research lies in the areas of software engineering and distributed systems. I specialize in methods for context-aware, self-adaptive applications and services, with emphasis on mobile and pervasive computing environments. I also experiment with Gamification as a means for student engagement and behavior change.

I maintain the School of Sciences' Seminars & Events blog.

I am also the founder of Code Cyprus which is a yearly event that aims to encourage high school students to learn programming.
