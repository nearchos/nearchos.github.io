I am a Professor at the University of Central Lancashire — Cyprus where I am the Head of the School of Sciences, an interdisciplinary team of academics working in computer science, engineering, mathematics, psychology, sports & exercise science, and media.

My research focuses on Mobile and Distributed Systems in the wider area of Software Engineering. I also study applications of Gamification as a means for behaviour change, and topics in Computer Science Education especially in the context of Code Cyprus.

I am the founder of Code Cyprus, a yearly event aiming to encourage high school students to learn programming.

Interested in a degree? Check out the Computing Programmes at computing.uclancyprus.ac.cy.
